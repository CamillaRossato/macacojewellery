Adopt Your Oyster


There is a market not only for traditional jewelry but also for
the adoption of small sustainable pearl farms through a specially created
subscription.

With the “adopt your own oyster” initiative, Macaco Jewels aims
to contribute to the project, which has the objective of carrying out a
controlled cultivation of oyster cultivation and the conservation of
marine fauna, particularly in the areas of the Pacific Ocean interested
in the process.

Thanks to this new way of cultivating pearls, in the last 25 years
oysters have started to repopulate, the local marine fauna has
expanded and has undergone a surprising regeneration and
repopulation.
