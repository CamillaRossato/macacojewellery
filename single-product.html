Antonella Clip/Stud

ANNE MANNS has been celebrated for its versatile designs that can be worn with unpierced ears. The Antonella Clip is one of them and can be worn along the helix of the ear and on the ear lobe. It looks great combined with a second clip. The designer Anne Manns perceives the world in search of natural forms and beautiful shapes, seeking her inspiration for the Antonella Clip from the shape of an aubergine. The Antonella Clip is minimal and understated enough to be worn everyday.
